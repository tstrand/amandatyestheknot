Our Story

February 27, 2016

The Proposal

Tyler spent weeks planning a secret proposal. With the ring hidden in his pocket, he tricked Amanda into revisting the Central Park Zoo where they had gone on their first date, 8 years earlier. Just as they passed the sea lions, Tyler got down on one knee and popped the question. She said yes!

When & Where

Ceremony and Reception

Our ceremony and reception will take place at 26 Bridge in Brooklyn, New York. Please plan to arrive between 5:30 and 6:00pm. The ceremony will begin at 6:15pm. Attire is formal but festive.

Accommodations

We've arranged room blocks at 3 hotels in New York City. The Dazzler is in Brooklyn, just a quick cab ride away from the venue. For reservations, call the hotel and use the group name 'Strand Loyola Wedding'. The Sheraton near Times Square is further from the venue, but a convenient place to stay if you plan to spend time in New York before or after the wedding. The Viceroy is in midtown, just a few blocks from Central Park.
