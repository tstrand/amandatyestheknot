Our Story

  • Young Love

    2005

    Tyler and Amanda met in middle school when they were just 11 years old. Amanda was a new student in 7th grade, and Tyler, quickly smitten, was eager to show her around.

  • High School Sweethearts

    2008

    After just 3 years of courtship, Tyler's crush was finally reciprocated, and Amanda agreed to go on a date at the Central Park zoo.

  • Heading West

    2010

    After graudating from Horace Mann, Tyler and Amanda headed west to Stanford. What a happy coincidence that they both decided to go to the same college!

  • Freshman Year

    2011

    College was full of new people and new experiences. Tyler joined the squash team while Amanda played soccer. They spent countless hours in the library, Tyler coding for his computer science classes while Amanda studied management science and engineering.

  • Study Abroad

    2012

    After 4 straight years spent by each other's side, Amanda headed off to study in Paris, and Tyler flew south to Brazil. Amidst endless nights of sobbing on long distance phone calls, they managed to find some time for fun.

  • Dancing Through Life

    2013

    After returning from abroad, Tyler and Amanda enjoyed their last few trimesters partying with friends in Sigma Nu and Pi Phi.

  • See ya Stanny!

    2014

    Before they new it, college was over. Tyler became a Product Manager working for a tech company in New York, and Amanda joined McKinsey & Company as a consultant.

  • Moving In

    2015

    After a year back home in New York, Tyler and Amanda packed their bags and moved to Brooklyn! Even though it was just across the river, it felt like the start of something big...

The Proposal

February 27, 2016

Tyler spent weeks planning a secret proposal. With the ring hidden in his pocket, he tricked Amanda into revisting the Central Park Zoo where they had gone on their first date, 8 years earlier. Just as they passed the sea lions, Tyler got down on one knee and popped the question. She said yes!
